How to Tie a Bowline

.

Steps Step 1 Step 2 Step 3
Lay the standing end of the rope in your left hand with your right hand holding the running end(very end of the rope) in your right. Create a small loop with the rope in your left hand. Take the end of the rope in your right hand and pass it up through the bottom of the loop you created in step one. (The rabbit comes out of the hole). Wrap the running end that you ran through the loop around the backside of the standing line and then run it back down through the loop you created. (Rabbit runs around the tree and back down the hole)

Beware: Do not use these or other types of knots for rock climbing or other dangerous activities without the supervision of a professional.

Knots when misused could result in injury or death
