The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

Introduction

I read the book The Curious Case of Benjamin Button by F. Scott Fitzgerald years ago, and while the book may seem rather corny at times, the book provides many thought provoking quotes on life, and what it means to live a full life. The quote from this book that I selected stands out to me because it highlights the importance of living life to the fullest, experiencing new things, and not being afraid to get out of your comfort zone to build a life that is fullfiling and meaningful.
